# Casos de prueba para gramática inglesa (english_grammar.txt)
# Formato: <oración> | <esperado: accept/reject>
# Líneas que empiezan con # son comentarios

# Casos válidos
she eats a cake with a fork | accept
the cat drinks the beer | accept
he eats | accept
she drinks juice | accept
the dog eats meat with a spoon | accept
he drinks beer | accept
the cat eats | accept

# Casos inválidos
fork eats the cat | reject
she the eats | reject
eats she | reject
the drinks | reject
